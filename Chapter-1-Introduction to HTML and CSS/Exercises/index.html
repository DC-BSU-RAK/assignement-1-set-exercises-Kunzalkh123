White chocolate cookies

White Chocolate cookies is one of the most easiest things to make and perfect. It is also a comfort food for many people. The white chocolate and the warm gooey cookie is a perfect snack for evening tea time.

Ingredients

  • Your would need the following :

  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 100 grams of brown sugar
  • 50 grams of white sugar
  • 100 gram of butter
  • 50 ml of melted butter
  • 2 room temprature eggs
  • 1 table spoon vanilla extract
  • 300 grams of white chocolate
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder

    • Preperation

  • Preheat the over at 250 degrees
  • In a large bowl your going to swift the dry ingredients first which is the flour and the baking soda and powder
  • After the dry ingredients are well combined add in a stick of butter the more butter you add the softer the cookie
  • In a seperate bowl mix in the melted butter with the sugars till its light and thick
  • Crack 2 eggs in to the sugar mix till it becomes pale and add in the vanilla mix well
  • Combine the dry mix to the wet and kneede it till it becomes some what a dough
  • Pour in the chocolate and kneede it again
  • Put the mix into the fridge for 30 mins
  • line your baking pan and rolls the dough into small circle
  • You will bake this for 25 mins

    • Tips

    • The cookie better must be rested for 30 mins otherwise it would not bake well
    • The fat quantity in the butter matters
    • Hand mix the dry and wet together because using a standmixer would over mix
    • Add flakey sea salt if you want for an extra touch
