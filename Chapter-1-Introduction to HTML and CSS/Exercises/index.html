White chocolate cookies

White Chocolate cookies is one of the most easiest things to make and perfect. It is also a comfort food for many people. The white chocolate and the warm gooey cookie is a perfect snack for evening tea time.

Ingredients

  • Your would need the following :

  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 100 grams of brown sugar
  • 50 grams of white sugar
  • 100 gram of butter
  • 50 ml of melted butter
  • 2 room temprature eggs
  • 1 table spoon vanilla extract
  • 300 grams of white chocolate
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
