White sauce pasta

White sauce pasta is one of the most easiest things to make and perfect. However, inorder to make it you must have compitment to it.

Ingredients

  • Your would need the following :

  • 1 cup of cheese you are free to use any kind
  • 500 grams of pasta (elbow type is preffered)
  • 200 gram of cream cheese
  • 100 gram of regular full fat cream
